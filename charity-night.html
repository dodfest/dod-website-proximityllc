The Danbury Benefit

An Evening for Our City

A formal benefit evening celebrating Danbury and the people who make it home. Cultural performances, spoken-word poetry, dance, recognition of community partners, and an exclusive look at A Home Video — our short documentary about modern Danbury.

The film is one moment of the night. The night itself is for Danbury — its residents, its leaders, its small businesses, and the neighborhoods that hold this city together. Every ticket is a vote of confidence in Danbury and the nonprofits serving it.

All net proceeds from ticket sales go directly to three local nonprofits serving Danbury's most vulnerable communities.

The Program

Your Evening

6:00 PM
Doors Open, Red Carpet, Cocktail Hour, Merch & VIP Pick-Up

Arrive on the red carpet at our historic downtown venue. Enjoy cocktails, mingle with fellow guests, and take photos at the sponsor backdrop. VIP ticket holders pick up their exclusive Day of Danbury t-shirts at the VIP desk in the lobby. Official Day of Danbury merch (tees, caps, jackets, and the festival zine) will be available for purchase in the lobby. Media coverage and networking as the evening begins.

7:00 PM
Guests Seated & Opening Remarks

Take your seats in our downtown Danbury venue. The evening officially opens with a welcome from the Day of Danbury team and recognition of sponsors and community partners.

7:15 PM
Live Cultural Performances

A curated lineup of live music, spoken-word poetry, and dance performances representing Danbury's diverse communities. A raffle drawing follows the final act.

8:00 PM
A Home Video — World Premiere

"Isn't it beautiful to be in Danbury?" The world premiere of A Home Video, a short documentary exploring Danbury, CT's vibrant multicultural life through interviews with small business owners, athletes, and community advocates who call this city home.

9:00 PM
Closing & Thank You

A final celebration and acknowledgment of sponsors, partners, and the community that made this inaugural evening possible. Join us tomorrow for Day 2 on Danbury Green.

A Special Moment of the Evening

"Isn't it beautiful to be in Danbury?"

A HOME VIDEO

A bonus to the evening: a short documentary about the Danbury of today, not the history books. Through intimate interviews with small business owners, professional athletes, and community advocates, A Home Video captures the pride, diversity, and spirit of a city where people from 43 countries live side by side.

Produced by Nebru Films. Premiering at The Danbury Benefit before the film hits festivals nationwide.
