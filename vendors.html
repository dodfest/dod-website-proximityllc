The Vendor Village

From authentic international cuisine to handmade artisan goods, the Vendor Village brings together the best of Danbury's multicultural identity in one place.

Limited vendor spaces are available across both festival days. Each vendor is selected to showcase the cultural diversity and entrepreneurial spirit of our community.
