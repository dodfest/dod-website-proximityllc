JUNE 27 & 28 · CITYCENTER DANBURY GREEN

Be Part of Day of Danbury

A free, two-day cultural festival and charity benefit. We need your hands to pull it off.

Questions? Call or text Pedro at 203-456-4787

Volunteer perk: a free custom shirt 👕

Please bring a plain white T-shirt and we will screen print it for free on site so you can wear it all day and show you're a part of the team. We will also be selling separate Day of Danbury shirts at the festival.

What we need help with

Pick a station and a time slot below. Most stations run in about two-hour rotations, and you can sign up for Saturday, Sunday, or both. The tent goes up Friday; the tables, chairs, slide, dunk tank, and snow cone machine all arrive Saturday at 8 AM, so early-morning setup help is gold. If you can come between 8 and 10 AM Saturday, please sign up.

Inflatable Obstacle Course 2-3 needed

Our 8-foot inflatable obstacle slide. The most important kid-safety station.

  • Three spots: one at the base, one in the middle, one at the top (2 minimum).
  • Form a line, one child at a time. Watch the entrance so kids come out before the new kid goes in, and keep everyone moving so the line never backs up.

Dunk Tank 2-3 needed

Run the dunk tank line and the throws.

  • Collect $5 for 12 throws. We have 6 balls, so each player throws 6, then you gather and reload for their second 6.
  • Keep the line moving and keep things safe and fun.

Face Painting (Kids) creative helpers

Got an artistic streak? Paint kids' faces and help make their day.

  • Let us know if you have your own paints and brushes, or bring your own.
  • Keep designs simple, fun, and quick so the line keeps moving.

Trash & Grounds Crew rotating

Keep CityCenter Danbury Green clean all day. CityCenter provides no staff, so this is on us.

  • Rotate around the Green collecting and emptying trash.
  • Trash drop is at the charter school / old Paul Mitchell lot.
  • Important: no toxic waste and no landfill material (dirt, etc.) in the trash.

Setup & Breakdown Crew Sat 8 AM · Sun 8 PM

Help set up Saturday morning and break everything down Sunday night (the tent goes up Friday).

  • Arriving 8 AM Saturday: 6 round tables, 6 six-foot tables, the inflatable obstacle course, the dunk tank, and the snow cone machine.
  • Set up tables and chairs under and around the 20' x 30' tent (seats 60).
  • Sunday 8 PM: full breakdown and cleanup, stage everything for pickup, final trash sweep. This is when we need the most hands.

Front Greeter & Info Booth 2-3 needed

Be the friendly face of the festival. Welcome guests, hand out schedules, answer questions, and point people to activities, food, and restrooms.

Ticket Sales 2 needed

Run the sales table for wristbands, activity tickets, and Charity Night tickets. Handle cash and card checkout and keep a tidy count.

Merch Booth 2 needed

Sell official Day of Danbury t-shirts. Manage sizes and checkout, and keep the table stocked and tidy.

Raffle Table 2 needed

Sell raffle tickets throughout the day and help run the drawing. Keep stubs organized and announce winners with the team.

Snow Cone & Popcorn 2-3 needed

Serve free snow cones and popcorn to the crowd. We will show you how to run the machines. Keep the station clean and stocked.

Wherever I'm Needed floater

Flexible helper. We will place you where the need is biggest that hour (booths, info, performances, lines).

Charity Night at the Palace

Saturday, June 27 · 6:30 PM arrival. A special, separate way to help.

What is Charity Night?

Charity Night, our Danbury Benefit, is a formal evening at The Palace Theatre with cultural performances, a red carpet arrival, a VIP reception, and the premiere of our short documentary A Home Video. It's a ticketed fundraiser, and proceeds go to three local nonprofits. Volunteer as a server and you get into the show for free.
