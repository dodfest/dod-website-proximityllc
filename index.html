About the Festival

One Weekend. Every Culture.
Our Danbury.

Danbury is the 7th most diverse city in the United States, home to families from 43 countries speaking 47 languages. Day of Danbury is a celebration of that richness. Bring a friend and experience something new together — a dish you've never tried, a dance you've never seen, a story you've never heard.

A two-day, free-admission cultural festival and ticketed formal charity event, Day of Danbury brings together every corner of our city on CityCenter Danbury Green and at the historic Palace Theater. All net proceeds support three local nonprofits: New American Dream Foundation, Center for Empowerment & Education, and Living Traditions Foundation Inc.

Beneficiary Organizations

Three Missions. One Purpose.

All net proceeds from Day of Danbury go directly to three nonprofits strengthening our community.

New American Dream Foundation

Empowering immigrant and first-generation communities through advocacy, resources, and opportunity.

Supporting the Senior Hot Meal Program, delivering monthly meals and companionship to 650+ seniors in Danbury's tax-credit housing communities.

Visit NADF →

Center for Empowerment & Education

Breaking the cycle of interpersonal violence through empowerment, education, and support.

The only state-certified provider of DV and sexual assault services across 11 towns, serving 30,000+ individuals annually with 24/7 crisis intervention.

Visit CEE →

Living Traditions Foundation Inc.

Preserving cultural heritage through arts, storytelling, and community programming.

As the event organizer, LTF empowers first-time entrepreneurs in hospitality and the arts while celebrating Danbury's cultural richness.

Visit LTF →
