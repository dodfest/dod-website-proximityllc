Where Your Money Goes

Net proceeds from all sources (donations, tickets, sponsorships, and vendor fees) go directly to three nonprofits strengthening our community.

New American Dream Foundation

Supporting the Senior Hot Meal Program, delivering meals and companionship to seniors in Danbury's tax-credit housing communities.

Visit NADF →

Center for Empowerment & Education

The only state-certified provider of DV and sexual assault services across 11 towns, with 24/7 crisis intervention.

Visit CEE →

Living Traditions Foundation

Preserving the stories and cultural heritage of Danbury's communities through arts, storytelling, and community programming.

Visit LTF →
